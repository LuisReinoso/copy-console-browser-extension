Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2024

Overview

Copy Console is a browser extension that operates entirely within your browser. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension operates completely locally in your browser.

  • No usage tracking
  • No personal information collection
  • No cookies or local storage usage
  • No analytics or tracking scripts

Console Log Access

The extension only accesses your browser's console logs when you explicitly click to copy them. These logs:

  • Are only processed locally in your browser
  • Are never transmitted to any external servers
  • Are only stored temporarily in your clipboard when you choose to copy them

Payment Processing

For the Pro version purchase, payment processing is handled by Lemon Squeezy. We only receive license key information to validate your purchase.

  • Payment information is handled securely by Lemon Squeezy
  • We do not store any payment details
  • Only license key validation is performed locally

For more information about how Lemon Squeezy handles your payment data, please refer to their privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

We will update this privacy policy as needed to ensure it accurately represents our practices. When we make significant changes, we will notify users through the extension or our website.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our GitHub issues page.
